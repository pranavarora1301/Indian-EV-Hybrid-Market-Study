## 📌 Key Findings

### EV Market
- 🏆 India's EV market hit **2.3 million units in CY 2025** — 8% of all new registrations (VAHAN/IESA)
- 🛵 **E-2W** dominate with 56.4% of EV sales; e-3W account for 35%
- 🚗 **E-4W** grew 85.1% YoY — Tata Motors leads but its share fell from 61% to 39% as Mahindra surged +396%
- 🗺️ **Uttar Pradesh** is India's largest EV state (18% of national sales); **Tripura** has highest EV-to-ICE ratio (18%)
- 📈 EV sales grew from ~50,000 units in 2016 to 2.3 million in 2025 — a **46× increase in under a decade**

### Hybrid Market
- Strong hybrid sales **more than doubled** in Q1 FY2026 (118% YoY growth)
- **Toyota India** holds 81% of the strong hybrid market; Maruti Suzuki growing rapidly (+263% YoY)
- Hybrid market CAGR of **~34.8% projected for FY 2025–2030**

### Policy
- **PM E-DRIVE** (₹10,900 Cr) replaces FAME-II — targets 25 lakh EVs and 72,300 new chargers by FY2028
- **ACC PLI** (₹18,100 Cr) targets 50 GWh domestic battery cell manufacturing
- Government target: **30% EV penetration of new vehicle sales by 2030** (EV30@30 commitment)

## ⚠️ Data Notes & Caveats

1. **VAHAN data excludes Telangana** and low-speed electric 2-wheelers (data limitations of the portal).
2. **CY vs FY difference**: Calendar Year (Jan–Dec) vs Financial Year (Apr–Mar). Data labels specify the period.
3. **Hybrid figures (FY 2021–2023)** are industry estimates as official VAHAN breakdown was not publicly available.
4. **Future projections** (2026–2030) are market estimates from research firms and NITI Aayog — not official government guarantees.
5. E-4W OEM share percentages for CY 2024 are approximations; CY 2025 figures from EVreporter analysis.
6. All monetary values in Indian Rupees (₹) unless marked with USD ($).
